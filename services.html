How to install?

These are the easy steps you can follow in order to install the ARGBga App:

1. Go to any of the parks described in the "Where to go?" section.

When you are at the park, follow these simple steps

2. Search for the QR located near the main monument of the park.

3. Scan the QR.

4. Finally you'll be redirected to the App Store/Play Store, where you will be able to download the app
